PVC Production

In 1997, I joined the German-Lithuanian company Gealan-Baltic. I did an internship in Germany for several months. When back to Lithuania, we started with equipment, pipeline installation, up to the launch of extrusion lines. During the 15 years of working as a Shift Manager, production volumes increased from 3 to 17 lines and the team from 13 to 100 employees.
